**Title: “When the Peel Gets the Slip”**

*Scene Set‑up*:  
A sunny downtown park, late afternoon. The concrete walkway glistens after a sudden drizzle, and a mischievous breeze rustles the leaves of the nearby oak tree. On this very path, two unlikely characters are about to collide in the most absurdly slapstick way imaginable.

---

### The Cast

- **Benny the Banana** – A ripe, yellow, anthropomorphic banana with a jaunty green stem‑hat, tiny sneakers, and a big‑eyed, goofy grin. He’s always in a hurry, darting around the park trying to beat his own “peel‑time” record.

- **Harold “Human‑Peel” Patel** – A lanky jogger who, after an ill‑advised costume contest, decided to dress up as a giant banana peel. He’s wrapped head‑to‑toe in a slick, faux‑rubber peel costume (bright yellow with the classic speckled pattern) and carries a tiny prop banana for “authenticity.” Unfortunately for Harold, the costume’s underside is as slippery as a real banana peel.

---
