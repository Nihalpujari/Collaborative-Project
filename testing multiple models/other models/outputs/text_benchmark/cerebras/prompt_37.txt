### The “Pop‑Fit” Business Suit – A Playful Take on Professionalism

| **Aspect** | **Details** |
|------------|-------------|
| **Concept** | A full‑body business suit (blazer, waistcoat, trousers, shirt, tie, and even shoes) constructed entirely from high‑grade, pop‑ready bubble‑wrap sheets. Every step, gesture, and breath triggers a crisp, satisfying “pop.” |
| **Materials** | <br>• **Premium Bubble‑Wrap** – 2‑mm thick, double‑bubble (two layers of air pockets) for extra durability and a richer sound.<br>• **Clear, Recyclable TPU Over‑coat** – A thin, flexible skin that protects the bubbles from premature rupture while still allowing the audible pop when pressure is applied.<br>
