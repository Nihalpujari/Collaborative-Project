**Scene Description – “A Sad Hotdog at a Fancy Restaurant”**

*Setting & Mood*  
The scene takes place in an elegant, high‑end restaurant, the kind of place where crystal chandeliers cast a soft, amber glow over polished mahogany tables. A crisp white linen tablecloth, perfectly ironed, covers the surface, and a single, gleaming silver fork and knife sit politely beside a petite porcelain soup bowl. A single, flickering tealight in a crystal goblet adds a touch of romance to the otherwise muted atmosphere. The background is a subtle blur of other tables, a maître d’ in a tuxedo, and the muted hum of polite conversation—nothing that draws attention away from the lone protagonist.

*The Protagonist – The Hotdog*  
At the center of the table sits a solitary hotdog, rendered with a mix of hyper‑realistic detail and a whimsical, almost cartoonish soul. Its bun is lightly toasted, golden‑brown, and shows a gentle, almost imperceptible crack that hints at the weight of its story. The sausage itself is perfectly cooked, a deep, savory brown that glistens with a thin sheen of oil, as if it’s still warm from the grill.
