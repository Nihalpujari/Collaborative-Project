**Title:** *Morning Elegance – A Fancy Owl Sipping Coffee*

**Scene Overview**  
The composition centers on a dignified, anthropomorphic owl perched on the arm of a vintage, mahogany armchair. The owl is impeccably dressed in a crisp, white dress shirt with a silk, midnight‑blue cravat tucked neatly at its throat. A gold pocket watch hangs from its left breast pocket, its chain catching a soft glint of morning light. Its feathers are a rich tapestry of chestnut and amber, ruffling slightly as it leans forward to take a delicate sip from a porcelain cup of steaming coffee.
