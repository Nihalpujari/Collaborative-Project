### **Scene: The Sith Lord’s Domestic Rage**
*(A dimly lit, slightly dusty living room. The hum of a high-powered vacuum roars to life as Darth Vader looms over a plush rug, cape billowing dramatically. The Force crackles with his irritation.)*

**Vader:** *(growling, mechanical breath echoing)* **"I find your lack of… cleanliness… disturbing."**

*(He ignites the vacuum with a dramatic flick of his wrist, the machine roaring like a TIE fighter engine. The rug *shudders* under the assault, fibers standing on end as if resisting the Dark Side’s pull.)*

**Vader:** *(seething)* **"You will be… *exterminated*."**

*(He *Force-chokes* the vacuum’s hose, bending it into a grotesque angle to reach a stubborn Cheeto crumb. The machine whines in protest, but Vader’s grip is unrelenting. A nearby cat watches in horror, tail puffed up like a tiny Sith apprentice.)*

**Vader:** *(muttering)* **"Pathetic. Even the *dust mites* have surrendered."**

*(A rogue Lego brick is sucked up with a *CRUNCH*. Vader pauses, then nods in grim satisfaction.)*

**Vader:** **"Good. *Now* we are making progress."**

*(The vacuum suddenly *explodes* in a shower of sparks—overloaded by Vader’s sheer intensity. He stands amidst the wreckage, cape singed, breathing heavily.)*

**Vader:** *(deadpan)* **"…I *hate* this rug."**

*(Cut to: Emperor Palpatine on a hologram, sipping tea.)*

**Palpatine:** *"Vader… why are you vacuuming?"*
**Vader:** *"…It was *your* turn to clean the Death Star’s floors."*
**Palpatine:** *(chuckling)* *"Ah. Carry on."*
