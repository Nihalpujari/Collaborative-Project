- **Eyes:** Large, luminous amber-gold eyes framed by round, wire-rimmed **reading glasses** perched on their hooked beak. The lenses magnify their pupils slightly, adding a touch of endearing nearsightedness. When they blink, their nictitating membranes sweep across like delicate vellum pages.

  - A **lace-trimmed handkerchief** tucked into their vest pocket, embroidered with their initials in an ornate script.
