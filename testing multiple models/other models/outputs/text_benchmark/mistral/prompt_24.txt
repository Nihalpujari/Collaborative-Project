### **Superman’s Painful Leg Waxing Session**
**Setting:** A trendy, upscale salon in Metropolis, where Lois Lane has dragged Clark Kent for a "self-care day" (much to his horror).

**The Scene:**
- **Clark’s Reluctance:** He’s already sweating bullets, trying to play it cool. *"Uh, Lois, I don’t think this is really my thing… maybe I’ll just… shave?"* But Lois insists—*"You’re getting the full treatment, babe. No half-measures!"*
- **The Esthetician’s Confusion:** The poor waxing technician, a sweet but no-nonsense woman named Maria, is baffled. *"Sir, your skin is… unusually smooth. And warm. And… are you glowing?"* Clark nervously laughs. *"Haha, yeah, uh, good genes!"*
- **The First Strip:** Maria slaps on the hot wax, presses the strip, and—**RIP.** Clark’s face contorts in agony, his eyes watering. *"OH COME ON!"* he yelps, gripping the chair. The salon goes silent. A nearby customer drops her latte.
- **The Aftermath:** Maria gasps. *"Sir… there’s no hair. Just… smooth, indestructible skin."* Clark, panting, realizes his mistake. *"Oh. Right. Kryptonian biology. Maybe I should’ve mentioned that?"*
- **The Super-Speed Recovery:** Before Maria can apologize, Clark’s skin is already healing. *"Uh… wow. That was fast."* Lois, trying not to laugh, pats his knee. *"Well, at least now we know waxing doesn’t work on you."*
- **The Bill:** Maria charges him double for the "special treatment." Clark pays without complaint, just grateful the ordeal is over.

**Epilogue:**
- Later, at the *Daily Planet*, Jimmy Olsen snaps a photo of Clark’s red, irritated legs (from the failed waxing) and teases him mercilessly. *"Dude, what happened to you?!"* Clark groans. *"Don’t ask."*
