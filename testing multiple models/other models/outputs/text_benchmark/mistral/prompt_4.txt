The desk is littered with tiny spreadsheets, a half-chewed pencil, and a framed photo of itself shaking paws with a very nervous-looking chipmunk intern.

In the background, a skyline of oak trees stretches into the distance, their leaves rustling with the sound of ringing phones and the distant chatter of a squirrel boardroom. Somewhere, a woodpecker taps out a Morse code version of *"Sell, sell, SELL."*
