**Scene Overview**

Imagine a soaring, sun‑kissed sky where the drama of early aviation meets the wild majesty of the avian world. A powerful golden‑brown eagle—its feathers gleaming with a subtle iridescence—clutches the control stick of a classic 1920s biplane. The eagle wears a snug, leather‑trimmed pilot’s headset, complete with copper‑tone ear cups and a thin, brass microphone that hangs like a tiny banner. Together, the bird and the aircraft cut a graceful, daring arc through fluffy, billowing clouds that glow with the soft pink‑orange hues of a late‑afternoon sun.
