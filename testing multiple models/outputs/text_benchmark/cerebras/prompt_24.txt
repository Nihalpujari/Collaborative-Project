**Title: The Wax of Krypton**

The neon sign of *Belle‑Belle’s Beauty Boutique* flickered in the early morning light, promising a “spa-tacular experience” for anyone brave enough to try their new “Kryptonite‑Infused Leg Wax.” Inside, the air was scented with lavender and a faint metallic tang that no one could quite place.

Superman—Clark Kent in his ordinary blue shirt and red tie—stood at the reception desk, trying to look as unremarkable as possible. The receptionist, a perky teenager named Jess, glanced up from her tablet and gave him a nervous smile.

> “Welcome to Belle‑Belle’s! Can I get your name, please?”

Clark cleared his throat, feeling a little embarrassed about the request.

> “Clark. Clark Kent.”  
> 
> “Great! You’re booked for a full-leg wax at 10:00 a.m. right now. Follow me.”

He followed Jess down a hallway lined with mirrors that reflected his iconic silhouette back at him—a cape draped over a borrowed trench coat. The sound of soft jazz filled the space, punctuated by the occasional sigh of the air conditioner.

The waxing room was a small, plush studio with a single reclined chair and a basket of wax pots. On the countertop, a label read: **“Kryptonite‑Infused Wax – 100% Natural, 0% Super‑Strength.”** Jess offered Clark a tiny glass of water and a choice between a lavender-scented mask or a charcoal one.

> “No thanks, just… the wax,” Clark replied, his tone flat but his mind racing with thoughts of the inevitable heat.
