**Scene Summary**

A mischievous gray‑brown squirrel dashes across a sun‑dappled city park, its tiny paws gripping a sleek skateboard. Perched atop its head is a battered, straw‑woven cowboy hat—downturned brim, a faded red band, and a feather tucked into the side. The squirrel’s bushy tail wiggles for balance, and its beady eyes sparkle with adventurous confidence. Around it, park benches, a few scattered acorns, and a distant graffiti‑tagged wall add urban flavor, while a few startled pigeons flutter away in the background.
