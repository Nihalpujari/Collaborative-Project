**Scene Overview**

A plump, happy pug lounges on a bright, oversized pool float, soaking up the sun. He’s sporting a pair of oversized, retro‑style sunglasses that sit just above his round, squinty eyes. The float is shaped like a giant, inflatable slice of watermelon (or you could swap for a flamingo, a donut, or a classic “inner tube” if you prefer), floating lazily on crystal‑clear turquoise water. The whole composition feels breezy, playful, and unmistakably summery.
