**🍕🛹 “Pepperoni Drop” – A Whimsical Visual‑Story Sketch**  

---

### 1️⃣ The Scene in Words  
Imagine a bright, sunny afternoon on a downtown skate park. The concrete handrail glints under the light, its metal edge as sleek as a surfboard’s nose. At the very top of the rail, perched precariously, is… a slice of pepperoni pizza.  

- **The Slice:** A perfectly triangular wedge, its crust golden‑brown with a delicate chew, the cheese stretched into a glossy, buttery veil. Pepperoni rounds are pepper‑spotted, glossy like tiny moons. A faint steam still rises, hinting at the pizza’s just‑baked warmth.  
- **The Skateboard:** Instead of wood and trucks, the slice’s own crust forms the board. The cheese acts as the deck’s “grip tape,” giving just enough friction for a smooth ride. Two tiny, invisible wheels (or perhaps a magical levitation) keep the slice gliding.  
- **The Motion:** With a daring wobble, the pizza leans into the rail, its cheese edge scraping briefly— *squeak!*—as it hooks onto the metal. The pepperoni islands spin lazily, like toppings on a carousel. The slice rolls forward, a buttery blur, gaining momentum, each second of the ride a buttery ballet.  
- **The Background:** A backdrop of graffiti‑tagged concrete, a few on‑lookers in hoodies, and a skate‑boarding dog
