**Scene Description – “Avocado Beats”**

*Setting*: A cozy, sun‑lit kitchen nook. A low, wooden counter holds a tiny drum kit that looks like it was built for a mouse—two mini snare drums, a petite bass drum, a couple of hi‑hat cymbals, and a tiny floor tom. The kit rests on a rug of leafy green “herb” fabric that matches the avocado’s skin.

*The Avocado*:  
- **Head (Pit)** – A dark, glossy seed that serves as the avocado’s “face.” Two tiny, black‑kissed eyes are drawn just above the pit, and a mischievous grin curves around it, showing a single, smooth “tooth” (a little leaf‑shaped blemish).  
- **Body** – The buttery‑green flesh of the avocado forms a rounded, plush torso, slightly squishy, perfect for a soft, springy bounce when it drums. A faint, natural vein pattern runs like a subtle shirt.  
- **Arms** – Two slender, flexible stems sprout from the top of the body, ending in leafy “hands.” The leaves are semi‑transparent, giving them a gentle, drum‑stick‑like feel. One arm holds a pair of miniature drumsticks made from slender twigs, each capped with a tiny piece of bark for a perfect grip.  
- **Legs** – Below the “waist,” the avocado tapers into a short, sturdy seed‑shaped base that sits comfortably on the drum kit. Two small, rounded “feet” (little bits of the avocado’s skin) rest on the bass drum’s rim, allowing the avocado to lean into the rhythm.

*Action*:  
- The avocado leans forward, its green torso bobbing with the beat.
