**A Slip‑Slide Spectacle: The Banana vs. the Human Banana Peel**

In the bustling metropolis of *Fruitopolis*, the streets are a kaleidoscope of colors—orchard‑yellow taxis, emerald‑green traffic lights, and the occasional splash of crimson strawberries crossing the boulevard. It’s a city where the ordinary is extraordinary, and today, the extraordinary is about to unfold.

---

### **The Protagonist: Benny the Banana**

Benny is no ordinary banana. He’s a ripe, slightly curved, golden‑yellow gentleman with a jaunty little stem hat perched atop his head. His peel is glossy, speckled with the faintest hint of brown freckles—a sign of perfect ripeness. Benny walks (well, rolls) on tiny, banana‑shaped wheels, humming a jaunty tune as he slides down the promenade, his eyes shielded by tiny sunglasses that reflect the neon sign of “Peel‑It Café.”

### **The Antagonist: Gary, the Human Banana Peel**
