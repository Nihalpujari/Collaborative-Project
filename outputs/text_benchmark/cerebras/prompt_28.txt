**Illustration Prompt – “Angry Taco Lifts Iron”**

---

**Scene Overview**  
A colossal, anthropomorphic taco stands in the middle of a bright, modern gym, its tortilla shell slightly shredded at the edges from a recent “wrath‑inducing” battle. The taco’s expression is ferocious—eyebrows furrowed, eyes narrowed, a steam‑wisp of anger curling from its “mouth” (filled with spicy salsa and cheese). Muscles made of tightly rolled lettuce, beef, and beans bulge as it hoists a massive barbell. The gym’s equipment—rack, mirrors, and motivational posters—adds realistic context, while the taco’s vivid colors contrast sharply with the steel and concrete surroundings.

---

### 1️⃣ Composition & Perspective
- **Camera Angle:** Low‑angle, slightly to the right of the taco, looking up. This emphasizes the taco’s towering size (≈12 ft tall) and gives the viewer a heroic‑yet comedic feel.  
- **Framing:** The taco is centered, occupying roughly 60 % of the vertical space. The barbell arcs above it, with the weight plates visible in the foreground, creating a dynamic diagonal line that leads the eye from the taco’s fists to the gym ceiling.  
- **Background:** A wall‑mounted mirror reflects the taco’s side profile, reinforcing the sense of scale and adding depth. A motivational banner reads “NO TORTILLA LEFT BEHIND!” in bold, distressed lettering.
