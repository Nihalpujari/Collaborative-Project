As the frog plays on, a gathering of curious pond creatures begins to assemble around the lilypad. A family of ducks swims by, quacking in time with the music, while a pair of fish peer out from beneath the surface, their scales shimmering in the sunlight. The frog, feeling like a true performer, begins to get into the zone, shredding out a face-melting solo that sends ripples through the water.
