(Darth Vader, dressed in his iconic black suit and helmet, stands in the center of a living room, a vacuum cleaner at his side. He gazes out at the rug before him, his breathing heavy in the silence.)

Darth Vader: (in a deep, ominous voice) This rug... is a rebel. It is dirty, it is unkempt. It must be punished.

(With a swift motion, Darth Vader grasps the vacuum cleaner's handle and flips the switch. The machine roars to life, and Vader begins to aggressively vacuum the rug, moving with a deliberate, menacing pace.)

Darth Vader: (gritting his teeth) You will be clean. You will be spotless. You will obey me.

(As he vacuums, Vader's movements become more and more aggressive, the vacuum cleaner bucking and jerking in his grasp. He attacks a particularly stubborn stain, the vacuum's suction growing stronger as he applies more pressure.)

Darth Vader: (snarling) Resistance is futile. The power of the dark side will not be denied.

(The vacuum cleaner's belt begins to smoke, and the machine starts to overheat, but Vader refuses to relent. He continues to vacuum, his anger and frustration fueling his actions.)

Darth Vader: (bellowing) I will not be defeated by a simple rug! I will not be defeated by dirt and dust!

(Just as it seems the vacuum cleaner is about to explode, Vader finally turns it off, standing victorious over the now-spotless rug. He surveys his handiwork, his chest heaving with exertion.)

Darth Vader: (in a deep, satisfied voice) The rug... is clean. The rebellion... is crushed.

(With a final, ominous glance around the room, Darth Vader turns and exits, leaving behind a trail of cleanliness and terror.)
