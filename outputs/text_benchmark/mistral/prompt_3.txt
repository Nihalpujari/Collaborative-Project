**Title: "Sky Sovereign"**

A **golden eagle** with wings outstretched in effortless dominance soars through a sea of fluffy cumulus clouds, its feathers ruffled by the wind. Perched confidently in the open cockpit of a **vintage biplane** (think 1920s–30s style, with wooden struts and a polished metal fuselage), the eagle grips the control stick with one taloned foot while the other rests on the throttle. A **leather pilot’s headset**, slightly askew, sits atop its regal head, the ear cups framing its piercing amber eyes. The goggles’ strap dangles loosely, as if the eagle had just adjusted them mid-flight.

The biplane’s propeller whirs, leaving a faint trail of mist in its wake, and the aircraft’s wings—painted with bold stripes or a vintage squadron insignia—cut through the clouds like a blade. Below, the earth is a patchwork of green and gold, barely visible through the haze. The eagle’s expression is one of **commanding focus**, a ruler of the skies who has traded talons for technology but remains untamed.

**Details to consider:**
- **Lighting:** Warm golden-hour sunlight filtering through the clouds, casting dramatic shadows on the eagle’s feathers and the plane’s wings.
- **Texture:** The contrast between the eagle’s sleek plumage and the biplane’s weathered wood/metal.
- **Action:** A slight tilt of the plane’s wings, as if banking into a turn, with the eagle’s tail feathers fanned for balance.
- **Extras:** A vintage compass or map tucked into the cockpit, or a scarf (like a WWI pilot’s) fluttering in the wind.
