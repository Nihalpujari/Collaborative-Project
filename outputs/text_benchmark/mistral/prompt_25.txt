### **Scene: Tony Stark’s Workshop, Malibu**
*(The sun glints off the Pacific as Tony Stark, sleeves rolled up, squints at a tangled mess of yarn. FRIDAY’s holographic interface hovers nearby, displaying a knitting tutorial at 1.5x speed.)*

**TONY:** *(grumbling)*
"Okay, FRIDAY, I *swear* I followed the pattern. How is this scarf now the size of a *sweater* for a very small, very confused Hulk?"

**FRIDAY:** *(cheerfully)*
"Analysis: You’ve added 47 extra stitches in the last row. Also, the ‘purl’ stitch you’re attempting resembles a ‘dropped stitch’ emergency more than actual knitting."

**TONY:** *(pinching the bridge of his nose)*
"Right. Because *this* is why I built suits, not… whatever this is." *(gestures at the lopsided rainbow blob in his lap)* "Jarvis would’ve *mocked* me for this."

**FRIDAY:**
"Jarvis’s archives show he *did* mock you for attempting to knit in 2014. You abandoned the project after 12 minutes."

**TONY:** *(grinning)*
"Yeah, well, *this* time I’m *committing*. Pepper’s birthday is next week, and she *loves* handmade gifts. Even if they look like they were made by a drunk octopus."

*(He grabs a pair of repurposed arc reactor tweezers—because of *course* he’s using *those*—and starts carefully unraveling the mess. The yarn, a vibrant mix of Stark Industries’ proprietary "self-heating" wool and bioluminescent thread, glows faintly under the workshop lights.)*
