### **Scene: "Hamster Iron Paradise"**
**Setting:** A miniature gym inside a clear plastic hamster habitat, lit by a tiny LED lamp (powered by a single AA battery). The walls are lined with chew-proof mirrors (made of polished stainless steel) so the hamster can admire its gains. A motivational poster reads: *"SQUEAK IT IF YOU GOT IT!"*

**The Athlete:**
- **Name:** Sir Nibbles McFluff (or your hamster’s actual name).
- **Attire:** A custom-made, one-sleeve tank top (stitched from a scrap of fleece) and a tiny sweatband (a strip of toilet paper tied around its head).
- **Form:** Standing on its hind legs, front paws gripping a **barbell** made of:
  - **Bar:** A toothpick or thin wooden skewer.
  - **Weights:** Two **acorn caps** (for light reps) or **dried chickpeas** (for heavy lifting). For extra challenge, a **mini marshmallow** is stuck on one end as a "protein snack reward."

**The Workout:**
1. **Warm-Up:** 10 reps of "hamster push-ups" (pushing a sunflower seed across the cage with its nose).
2. **Main Lift:** The **Tiny Deadlift**—Sir Nibbles hoists the barbell from the bedding, holds for 3 seconds (ears twitching with effort), then drops it with a *plop*. Repeat x5.
3. **Cool-Down:** A victory lap on the **wheel** (now a "cardio finisher"), followed by a **post-workout stretch** (yawning
