Here’s a fun and creative way to visualize your hamster gym scene—complete with tiny details to make it feel like a real (and adorable) workout session:

---

### **Scene: "Hamster Iron Paradise"**
**Location:** A miniature gym inside a clear plastic hamster habitat, lit by a tiny LED lamp (powered by a single AA battery). The walls are lined with chew-proof mirrors (made of polished stainless steel) so the hamster can admire its gains.

#### **The Athlete:**
- **Name:** *Sir Fluffington the Mighty* (or *Gymmy McWheelspin*, if you prefer).
- **Attire:** A custom-made, breathable mesh tank top (stitched from a scrap of old sock) and a sweatband (a strip of toilet paper tied around its forehead).
- **Gloves:** Optional—tiny fingerless gloves cut from a latex glove to prevent calluses (or just for the aesthetic).

#### **The Equipment:**
1. **The Barbell:**
   - **Weight:** Two acorn caps (filled with sand or birdseed) threaded onto a wooden skewer (sanded smooth to avoid splinters).
   - **Grip:** The skewer is wrapped in medical tape for traction.
   - **Total Weight:** ~5 grams (equivalent to a human lifting ~1,000 lbs, adjusted for hamster proportions).

2. **The Bench:**
   - A flat rock or a small wooden block (sterilized and sanded) for incline presses.

3. **Accessories:**
   - **Chalk:** A pinch of cornstarch in a bottle cap for grip.
   - **Spotter:** A plush toy mouse (or a real mouse, if you’re feeling bold) cheering from the sidelines.
   - **Water Bottle:** A tiny thimble with a dropper for hydration.

#### **The Workout:**
- **Warm-Up:** 5 minutes of wheel sprints (mandatory to avoid "hamstring" strains).
- **Exercise 1:** *Acorn Curl* – 3 sets of 12 reps (hamster curls the barbell to its chest, grunting dramatically).
- **Exercise 2:** *Overhead Press* – 3 sets of 8 reps (pushing the barbell up while standing on hind legs, like a tiny Atlas).
