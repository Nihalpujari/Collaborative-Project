**The Dapper Owl’s Morning Ritual**
Perched on a velvet-cushioned stool at a tiny café table, the owl adjusts his gold-rimmed monocle with one talon, the other delicately cradling a porcelain cup of steaming coffee. His feathers are impeccably groomed—chestnut brown with silver-tipped wings, as if he’s just returned from a soirée at the *Grand Aviary Ball*. A silk cravat (tied in a perfect Windsor knot) peeks out from beneath his waistcoat, and his tiny spectacles fog slightly from the coffee’s warmth.

The cup itself is a work of art: hand-painted with tiny owls mid-flight, the saucer etched with constellations. A curl of steam rises, carrying the scent of cinnamon and dark roast, and the owl inhales deeply before taking a sip—*too* hot, of course, because even owls are impatient for their caffeine. His eyes widen, pupils dilating in delight, and he sets the cup down with a satisfied *hoo*.

In the background, a barista (a very patient squirrel) polishes a silver tray, while a sparrow at the next table whispers to her friend, *"Is that the Duke of Nocturne? I heard he takes his coffee with a single sugar cube and a sonnet."*
