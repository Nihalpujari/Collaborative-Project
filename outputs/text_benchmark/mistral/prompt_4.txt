**Title: "The Squirrel CEO"**

A sleek, chestnut-furred squirrel stands upright on a gnarled oak branch, its tiny paws gripping a **gold-plated smartphone** that glints in the dappled sunlight. The squirrel is dressed in a **tailored navy pinstripe suit**, complete with a **red power tie** (slightly askew, as if adjusted in haste) and **polished black loafers** that dangle just above the bark. Its bushy tail is puffed up like a corporate flag, twitching with barely contained aggression.

The squirrel’s **beady black eyes** are narrowed, its **whiskers bristling** as it **barks into the phone** with the intensity of a Wall Street tycoon mid-hostile takeover. Its tiny mouth is open wide, revealing **razor-sharp incisors**—not for nuts, but for **shredding quarterly reports**. The phone’s screen glows with what appears to be a **stock ticker**, a **hostile email**, or perhaps a **vengeful Yelp review** of a rival acorn supplier.

In the background:
- A **tiny briefcase** (monogrammed *"SQRL, Inc."*) lies open, spilling out **miniature contracts** and a **single, half-eaten walnut** (the squirrel’s "power snack").
- A **squirrel intern** (wearing a tiny headset) cowers behind a leaf, holding a **clipboard** with the words *"DO NOT DISTURB"* scribbled in acorn juice.
- A **pigeon in a janitor’s uniform** sweeps up discarded **stock certificates** near the base of the tree, muttering, *"Not my circus, not my nuts."*

**Caption options:**
- *"When the market opens and you’re still holding last quarter’s acorns."*
