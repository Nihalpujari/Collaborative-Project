### **Scene: A Knight vs. the Smartphone**
**Setting:** A dimly lit stone chamber in a castle. Sir Godfrey the Unyielding, a grizzled knight in full plate armor, stares in bewilderment at a glowing rectangle in his gauntleted hands. His squire, a tech-savvy teenager from the 21st century (time-traveling or magically summoned), watches in exasperation.

- **The Knight’s Reaction:**

  - He holds the phone at arm’s length, as if it might explode.
  - Taps the screen with his mailed finger—nothing happens. *"Curses! The witchery resists my touch!"*
  - Tries to pry it open with his dagger. *"Perhaps the magic lies within!"* (Squire screams: *"NO! THAT’S THE BATTERY!"*)
