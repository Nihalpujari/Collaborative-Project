**Title: *Paws on the Decks***

The neon lights pulse in time with the bass, casting a kaleidoscope of colors over the writhing crowd. At the center of it all, perched on a towering DJ booth, is **DJ Whiskertron**—a sleek black cat with emerald-green eyes, headphones snug over his ears (one slightly askew, because *cool*). His tail flicks in rhythm as he scratches a vinyl record with one paw, the other deftly adjusting the mixer knobs with the precision of a feline surgeon.

The crowd—humans, a few dogs in sunglasses, even a raccoon in a tracksuit—loses their minds. A tabby in the VIP section tosses a handful of catnip into the air like confetti. A bass drop hits, and the entire club *vibrates* with the collective purr of approval.

DJ Whiskertron’s ears twitch as he leans into the mic: *"Meow’s the time to lose your mind…"* The crowd roars. The bassline *hits*. The night is *young*.
