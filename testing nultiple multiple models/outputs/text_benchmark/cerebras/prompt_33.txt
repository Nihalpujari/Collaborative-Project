**Scene Description – “The Fry‑Selfie”**

Imagine a classic fast‑food cardboard container, its blue‑white checkered lid slightly ajar. Inside, a lively crowd of golden‑cooked french fries has gathered in a semi‑circle, each one “posed” as if ready for a photo‑op. The fries—some crisped to a perfect amber, others still a little soft‑on‑the‑inside—are anthropomorphized with tiny, playful expressions:

- **The “Leader” Fry** (the longest, slightly curved) holds an old‑school smartphone up at arm’s length, its cracked screen reflecting the gleam of the fries’ oil. The phone is angled so the whole box fits into the frame.
- **The Self‑ie‑Gurus**: A trio of short, stubby fries lean forward, their tips forming a makeshift “cheek” gesture, as if smiling for the camera.
- **The “Shy” Fry** peeks from behind a stack of its comrades, blushing a deeper golden hue.
- **The “Snack‑Star” Fry** sports a tiny ketchup packet as a hat, perched jauntily on its “head,” while a dollop of mustard smiles from the side.
- **The “Background” Props**: A crumpled napkin, a half‑eaten pickle, and a stray onion ring act as accessories, adding texture to the scene.
