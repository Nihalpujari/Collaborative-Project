**Scene Snapshot**

A battered, straw‑colored cowboy hat—worn low over one ear—rests humorously atop the squirrel’s fluffy head, the brim brushing against its whiskered cheeks. The hat’s dark felt band is decorated with a tiny silver star, giving it that classic Western flair.

The skateboard wheels spin lazily, kicking up a puff of dust as the squirrel rolls forward across a sun‑dappled park pathway. Its bushy tail flicks back and forth for balance, and its bright black eyes are wide with exhilaration, scanning the world ahead. A few acorns—tucked into a tiny saddle‑like pouch strapped to the skateboard’s underside—occasionally tumble out, bouncing in rhythm with each roll.

Sunlight catches the hat’s brim, casting a playful shadow on the squirrel’s cheek, while the surrounding foliage blurs into a soft green backdrop. In the distance, a distant park bench and a couple of pigeons watch the unlikely rider with bemused curiosity.
