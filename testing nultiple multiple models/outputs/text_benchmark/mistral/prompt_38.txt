### **Title: *The Great TP Heist***
**Genre:** Urban Absurdist Comedy / Silent Film (with a *Mission: Impossible* soundtrack)

#### **Act 1: The Escape**
- **The Setup:** A delivery truck’s back door swings open on a busy downtown street. A single, unassuming toilet paper roll (let’s call it *Rollie*) tumbles out, landing with a soft *thud* on the pavement. It’s free. It’s terrified. It’s *alive*.
- **The Spark:** A gust of wind (or the ghost of a mischievous janitor) sends Rollie rolling—first hesitantly, then with purpose. It dodges a pigeon’s peck, weaves between a tourist’s legs, and gains speed.
- **The Chase Begins:** A street vendor spots it and lunges. *Rollie* swerves. A dog gives chase. *Rollie* outmaneuvers it. A toddler in a stroller points and screams, *"MAMA, IT’S A GHOST!"*

#### **Act 2: The Urban Obstacle Course**
- **The Sewer Grate:** Rollie teeters on the edge of a storm drain. The crowd holds its breath. A gust of wind (or divine intervention) saves it. *Rollie* rolls on.
- **The Skateboarder:** A teen on a skateboard tries to "grind" Rollie like a rail. *Rollie* flips, lands upright, and *accelerates*.
- **The Food Cart:** A hot dog vendor’s cart blocks the path. *Rollie* takes a sharp left, narrowly avoiding a mustard bath. The vendor yells, *"THAT’S MY LUCKY ROLL!"*
- **The Protest:** A group of activists (holding signs like *"FLUSH THE PATRIARCHY"*) part like the Red Sea as *Rollie* barrels through. One drops their megaphone in awe.
