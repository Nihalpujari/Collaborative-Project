Imagine a **retro CRT monitor** (or a sleek modern one) with a pair of **oversized, comically thick reading glasses** perched on its "face." The screen displays a **glitchy, pixelated "confused" expression**, like:
- **Eyes:** Two mismatched, blinking question marks (`? ?`) or a single squinting emoji (`¯\_(ツ)_/¯`).
- **Mouth:** A wavy line (`~`) or a tilted underscore (`_`) to show uncertainty.
- **Background:** A desktop wallpaper of a **maze** or a **404 error screen** for extra humor.

The glasses are **crooked**, one lens fogged up, and the monitor’s power light flickers nervously.
